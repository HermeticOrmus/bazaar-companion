Peacewrought Mastery

MAK S-TIER

Core Mechanic

Destroy to Regenerate. Peacewrought destroys the item to its left, gaining Regen equal to the item's tier value. Higher tier items = more Regen = more sustain and damage.

Key Stats

Regen Haste CDR Crit

Economy Tip

Peacewrought grants 2/4/6/8 Gold on destroy based on tier. Use with Catalyst generators (Aludel, Mortar & Pestle) for infinite value.

Enchantment Guide

Choose the right enchantment for your situation

✕ +50% Crit Chance BEST
Massive damage boost. Best overall choice for damage scaling.
❄ Freeze GREAT
Control enemies while you scale. Excellent vs combo builds.
↯ Haste GREAT
More speed = more triggers = more Regen = more damage.
♥ Heal based on Regen GOOD
Synergizes with Peacewrought's Regen stacking. Good sustain.
◈ Shield based on Regen GOOD
Defensive option. Better vs burst damage builds.
⚔ Damage based on Regen SITUATIONAL
Stack with Staff of the Moose for mega damage. Requires setup.
✦ Immunity NICHE
Anti-Freeze tech. Take if facing heavy Freeze meta.

Monster Encounter Strategy

Navigate PvE fights to maximize value

✓ FARM
Slow monsters with high HP. More time to stack Regen and trigger Peacewrought multiple times. You out-sustain them.
✓ FARM
Poison monsters. Self-poison synergy exists in Mak's kit. Turn their damage into your advantage.
! CAUTION
Freeze monsters. If they freeze Peacewrought, you lose your engine. Position carefully or avoid if under-leveled.
✕ AVOID
Burst damage monsters early. Before Peacewrought gets rolling, fast burst can kill you. Wait until you have sustain.

Positioning Tips

  • Peacewrought destroys the item directly to its LEFT - always have a feeder there.
  • Keep catalyst generators far from Peacewrought to avoid accidental destruction.
  • Staff of the Moose should be adjacent to benefit from Regen doubling.
  • Position away from enemy Freeze targeting (usually first/last slots).
