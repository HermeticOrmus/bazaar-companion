## Hero Meta Summary

### Vanessa
**Tier**: S-Tier (Most versatile hero)
**Top builds**: Powder Keg, Slow Burn, Full Ammo Aggro, Anchor

**Key synergies**:
- Oni Mask offers strongest Burn scaling (needs reliable Crit)
- Tracer Fire + Incendiary Rounds spam
- Can pivot to Lighthouse build
- New items: Flame Signal, Hate Leech, Scimitar of the Deep, Vampire Squid

**Patch 9.0 changes**:
- Buffs: Chum (crit 4-10% → 3-12%), Honing Steel (CD 5→4, dmg 8-20 → 6-24), Ramrod (+10-30% Crit on reload, CD 5→3), Slumbering Primordial (multicast 1→4)
- Nerfs: Port (cost doubled), Shot Glasses (ammo 2-4 → 1-3), Switchblade (poison/burn bonus +3 → +1)

### Stelle
**Tier**: S-Tier (Lightning Rod), A-Tier (others)
**Top builds**: Lightning Rod, Space Laser, Stellar Swallowtail, Skyliner

**Key synergies**:
- Flying archetype with Stellar Swallowtail
- Caracara + Aerial Turret opener for speed/damage
- Gyro Gunsight for additional damage
- Headset kickstarts items when gaining Flying
- Clockwork Disc for long-term scaling

### Jules
**Tier**: A-Tier (Newest hero)
**Top builds**: Burn, Giant Lollipop, Freezer

**Key mechanics**:
- Heated/Chilled mechanic
- 60+ food/cooking items
- Stove/Cooler socket RNG affects build viability
- Requires 2nd Stove (Level 9) for Burn builds

**Build notes**:
- Burn: Hot Box + Imu core, or Food spam (Grill, Feast, Gingerbread House)
- Giant Lollipop: Dishwasher synergy, Excellent Vintage for quest, Cutting Board/Bubblegum Floor alternatives
- Extremely contextual positioning due to Stove/Cooler RNG

### Dooley
**Tier**: B-Tier (Post-nerf but still viable)
**Top builds**: Dooltron, Critical Core, Launcher Core, Poison/Burn

**Key changes**:
- Core rework knocked Dooltron down from S-tier
- Philosophy shift: in-combat scaling instead of permanent scaling
- Poison Dooley emerging post-9.0 (Ice 9000 buffed, multiple 10-wins Dec 3-4)
- Still strong due to multiple viable options at all stages

**Patch 9.0 changes**:
- New item: Micro Mach
- Buffs: Combat Core (dmg/shield 50-75 → 50-100), Ice 9000 (poison 20-30 → 20-40), Weakpoint Detector (5-20 → 5-40)
- Nerfs: Cool LEDs (Bronze→Silver), Temporal Navigator (CD 6→9-6, haste 1-4 → 2 fixed)

### Pygmalien
**Tier**: B+Tier
**Top builds**: Cash Cannon, Heal, Lemonade Stand, Jabalian Drum

**Key synergies**:
- Economy builds still viable
- Shield stacking strong

**Patch 9.0 changes**:
- Buffs: Abacus (+20-40 combat value), ATM (shield multiplier doubled), Boomerang (dmg 20-50 → 20-80), Gavel (200 → 100-300), Hatchet (CD 6→4)
- Nerfs: Showcase (dmg 20-60 → 10-40), Streaming Setup (reworked to +1 Toys multicast)

### Mak
**Tier**: S-Tier (Poppy Field), A-Tier (others)
**Top builds**: Poppy Field, Magnus' Femur, Peacewrought

**Key synergies**:
- Poppy Field + Spider Mace = infinite weapon loops
- Magnus' Femur + Incense = Slow-based scaling
- Peacewrought + Staff of the Moose = Regen stacking
- Runic Potion is staple for any weapon build
- New items: Book of Secrets, Spider Fern

**Patch 9.0 changes**:
- Buffs: Athanor (burn 12-20 → 15-25), Ice Claw (expanded to Silver, dmg 50-100 → 30-120)
- Nerfs: Runic Great Axe (Silver/Gold only, lifesteal crit 100% → 50-100%), Tome of Yyahan (enchantments removed)

## Patch 9.0 Key Changes

### Philosophy Shift
Devs continue moving away from permanent/out-of-combat scaling → in-combat scaling
- Less early build commitment
- Fewer un-fun snowball matchups
- Multiple items changed from "any adjacent item" triggers to "specific item type" conditions

### Major Mechanic Changes
- Enchantment reworks across multiple items
- Crit chance implementations standardized across Ray and Shield items
- Cooldown reductions on: Ramrod, Hatchet, Defense Matrix, DJ Circuit Breaker, Cooling Fan
