## Hero Meta Summary

### Vanessa
**Top builds**: Slow Burn, Powder Keg, Full Ammo Aggro, Anchor
- Oni Mask offers strongest Burn scaling (needs Crit)
- Tracer Fire + Incendiary Rounds spam
- Can pivot to Lighthouse build

### Dooley
**Top builds**: Dooltron, Critical Core, Launcher Core
- Core rework knocked Dooltron down from S-tier
- Still strong due to multiple viable options at all stages
- Focus on in-combat scaling now (not permanent)

### Pygmalien
**Top builds**: Cash Cannon, Heal, Lemonade Stand, Jabalian Drum
- Economy builds still viable
- Shield stacking strong

### Mak
**Top builds**: Peacewrought, Self Poison, Magic Carpet
- Potion/Poison focus
- New items: Foul Mushroom, Rainbow Staff

## Patch 8.0 Key Changes

### Philosophy Shift
Devs moving away from permanent/out-of-combat scaling → in-combat scaling
- Less early build commitment
- Fewer un-fun snowball matchups

### Major Nerfs
- **Dooley Dinosaur build**: Now in-combat scaling only
- **Obsidian Enchantment**: No longer gives Lifesteal, now converts to Weapon

### Buffs
- **Vanessa weapons**: CDR improvements (Cannon 5→4, Powder Horn 5→4)
- **Vanessa damage**: Bladed Hoverboard, Submersible buffed

### New Content
- **Jules**: 60+ new food/cooking items, Heated/Chilled mechanic
- **Stelle**: 5 new vehicle items
- **Mak**: New items added
